Hey there! As you probably can tell already, I'm Amatzhukaze.

Graphic design began as a hobby for me at the end of 2015 when I became interested in skinning for the rhythm game, osu!. It has been a long journey, from paint.NET and eventually to Photoshop, but every moment has been an exciting learning opportunity. Even today, I continue to expand my skill set, gaining new ideas and insights in the way I can utilize these tools to create awesome products!

In April 2017, a friend of mine asked me to customize his Twitch stream and profile. I decided to give it a spin, and found that I really loved the process. Not only was it a great way to practice my skills and continue to improve, but it also felt really satisfying to see someone's stream transform into a work of art. Soon after, I opened this service, and through the many hours of fun, I believe I have created something I take pride in.

Currently, I am learning more about vector graphics and tools like Illustrator, as they are very lightweight compared to rasterized work, and I want to see if they can be incorporated into my works. I also plan to learn about animations and visual effects and their applications in my products and beyond. Finally, I want to expand beyond Twitch, so if you have any special requests, I would gladly take them!
